3. Information We Collect

a) Device & Identity

✅ We do NOT require registration, login, or any personally identifiable information to use core Bible features. AI chat uses an anonymous session — no name, email, or password required.

b) Push Notification Token (FCM)

To deliver "Daily Verse" push notifications, we collect your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token. This token is device-specific, anonymised, and stored securely in our Supabase database. It is used solely to deliver notifications you have opted into.

c) Crash Reports & Analytics

  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect anonymised crash reports (device type, OS version, stack trace). No personal information is included.
  • We use Firebase Analytics to track app usage, including screen views, screen duration, quiz completion rates, and custom feature usage, to help us understand how users navigate the app and to improve our features. This data is fully anonymised.
  • Google AdMob may collect anonymised device identifiers and advertising IDs to serve relevant advertisements. See Section 5.

d) Background Task Scheduling

The App uses Android WorkManager / local scheduling to run reminders such as Daily Verse and engagement notifications. These tasks run on your device and are used only to deliver opted-in reminders. You can disable notifications any time in App Settings.

e) Media Saved to Gallery

When you choose to save a verse card image or video to your device gallery, the App requests access to your media storage. We only write the file you explicitly requested — we do not read, scan, or upload any existing media from your gallery.

f) User Activity & Streak Data

The App locally tracks your daily usage streak (consecutive days of opening the App) and quiz completion status to power the Engagement Strip on the home screen. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

g) AI Chat

Your text query is sent to our backend (Supabase Edge Function) to generate a response. The message is processed transiently and is not stored server-side after the response is returned. Chat history is stored on-device for app functionality only. We do not sell your personal data.

h) Audio Bible & Screen Wake-Lock

When using the Audio Bible feature, the App requests a temporary screen wake-lock to keep your device display on while scripture is being read aloud. This wake-lock is automatically released when audio playback stops or is paused. No data is collected or transmitted as part of this feature.

i) Song Favourites & Local Preferences

When you star a song as a favourite, the selected song ID is saved exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data is never uploaded to any server, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than displaying your personal favourites list within the App. Favourites are removed when you clear app data or uninstall the App.

j) In-Memory Song Title Cache

When you open the Songs section, the App fetches all song titles from our Supabase database and stores them temporarily in device memory (RAM) for the duration of your current session. This allows instant local search without repeated network requests. This in-memory data is cleared automatically when the App is closed or the session ends and is never written to disk.

k) Quiz Question Deck (Local Progress Tracking)

To ensure quiz questions are never repeated across sessions, the App stores a locally shuffled list of question IDs and a cursor position on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data (approximately 20–30 KB) is kept entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party. The deck is automatically reshuffled with a new random order once all questions have been seen.

l) Quiz Reward Economy (Coins, XP, Lives, Power-ups)

The App maintains a local reward system to track your quiz progress. The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Coins & XP: Accumulated from correct quiz answers. Used to unlock hints and power-ups within the App.
  • Lives (பெலன்கள்): A limited play currency that regenerates over time or is restored via rewarded ads. Tracked locally.
  • Power-ups (50/50, Hint, Freeze, Shield): Earned or purchased with coins. Inventory counts stored locally.
  • Level progression & stars: Your current quiz level, unlocked levels, and star ratings per level.
  • Daily challenge completion: Whether you have completed the daily challenge today (timestamp stored locally).
  • Streak & badges: Consecutive quiz play days and earned achievement badges.
✅ All quiz reward data is local-only. None of these values are sent to our servers, used for advertising targeting, or shared with any third party.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • FCM token: To send opted-in push notifications (Daily Verse and reminders).
  • Crash data: To diagnose and fix app issues.
  • Ad identifiers: To serve advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • Gallery permission: Only to save verse card images/videos you explicitly export.
  • Local streak/quiz data: To display your streak counter and progress features in the app.
  • Wake-lock: To keep the screen on during Audio Bible playback only.
  • Song Favourites: To remember and show your starred songs across sessions, stored only on your device.
  • Quiz Deck & Cursor: To track which quiz questions you have seen and serve fresh, non-repeating questions, stored only on your device.
  • Quiz Rewards (Coins, XP, Lives, Power-ups): To power the in-app gamification system, stored only on your device.

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App serves advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to serve contextually relevant ads. Ads shown include:

  • Banner ads — displayed on quiz and home screens.
  • Interstitial ads — shown at natural break points (e.g. between quiz levels).
  • Rewarded ads — shown only on your explicit request to earn lives or coins.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation, or by resetting your Advertising ID.

AdMob's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Permissions We Request

  • INTERNET: Required for AI chat, push notifications, and downloading media templates.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required on Android 13+ to show Daily Verse notifications.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: Required to select background images or videos from your gallery for verse cards.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Required to re-register WorkManager background tasks after device restart.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Required to keep the screen active during Audio Bible playback. Released automatically when playback ends.
✅ All permissions are requested only when needed and only with your explicit consent. No permission is used for any purpose beyond its stated function.

10. Account & Data Deletion

The App does not require account creation. However, the following server-side data can be deleted upon request:

  • FCM notification token — stored in our Supabase database to enable push notifications.

To request deletion:

  1. Email oswaldstack@gmail.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your approximate device model and the date you last received a notification (to help us identify your token).
  3. We will delete the record within 14 days and confirm by reply.

All local data (quiz progress, coins, XP, lives, song favourites, reading streaks) is stored only on your device and is permanently deleted when you clear app data or uninstall the App.

11. Data Retention

  • FCM tokens are retained until you uninstall the App or request deletion.
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days by Firebase.
  • AI chat queries are not retained server-side after a response is returned.
  • Local streak, quiz data, quiz deck progress, reward economy data, song favourites, and song title cache are removed when you clear app data or uninstall the App.

12. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures including HTTPS encryption for all network communication, Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS) for database protection, and no plaintext storage of sensitive credentials.
